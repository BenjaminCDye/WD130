Why do I write?

I like to write because it gives me an escape, a way to express myself, my feelings and identity. It is through my characters that I learn who I am. Through my stories, I can make sense of my feelings and anxieties. And with these stories, I can overcome them.

How can you be involved?

You can send a message down below to provide commentary, to reach out to me regarding a work position, or even if you just have a question.
