Why I like to Take Pictures

Pictures, I feel, are a way to solidify a moment of time. An event that will not be seen ever again by any person on the earth. Pictures allow us to recreate, and relive those moments.

Why I Like to Make Video

With video, you can capture more than just a moment in time. You can capture a feeling, an experience, a story. Just like how I enjoy writing stories, I feel like video has the same power to bring this construction, an idea, and make it real, if just for a moment.
