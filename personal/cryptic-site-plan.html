Overview

Purpose

This is a portfolio dedicated to displaying my work and projects, including Written work and stories, and my photography collection. The purpose of this website is to showcase my talents and skills for potential employers to see and judge for themselves. I do a lot of fictional writing, and this website is a simple way to access my work. I also love pictures, and I want to show that I have a good eye for noticing and capturing beauty. Are you looking for someone with artistic talent and that has the skills to utilize visual design space? Then take a look around my site and see for yourself that I have the experience and talents you are looking for.

Audience

To future employers or potential networking contacts. I want to reach creative directors, movie companies, photography studios. Anyone I can get to that appreciates the arts and fictional content and stories.
